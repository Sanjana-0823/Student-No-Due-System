Student Nodue Certificate System

Project Description

Abstract

Student No Due Certificate Tool is aimed to develop an application based on providing a No Due Certificate (NDC) to the students by all the department faculties. This system reduces the manual time consumption for the students and faculties. There is no required for the students to stand in front of department to get no due certificate but instead it can be done online through this application. This system also reduces the burden of the faculties and this make easier for all the departmental faculties to check the status of the students and report it accordingly, instead of recording it manually. Therefore, this application is focusing on developing a no due certificate which will generate computerized no due form showing the dues status.
